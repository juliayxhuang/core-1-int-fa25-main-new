Welcome to Ming Wong!

61 Mott St

Ming Wong is a cozy spot tucked away on Mott Street in Chinatown, NYC. It’s the kind of place that doesn’t try too hard — just really good Cantonese food that hits the spot every time. They’re known for their roast meats like BBQ pork, soy sauce chicken, and crispy duck, all piled over rice or noodles in that classic Chinatown style.

The vibe is simple and welcoming, the kind of restaurant locals actually go to — no frills, just solid food and fast service. Whether you’re grabbing takeout or sitting down for a quick meal, Ming Wong feels like one of those reliable neighborhood staples that never disappoints.
